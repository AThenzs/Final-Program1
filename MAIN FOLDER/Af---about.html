In the charming town of Harmonyville, there stood a legendary barbershop called "The Golden Cut" Founded long ago by the skilled barber Ninh Duong, it became a haven of community and music. When a young boy named Peter Griffin discovered his passion for both grooming and music, Ninh mentored him, passing down the tradition. Today, "The Golden Cut" thrives as a symbol of timeless harmony, where generations come to experience the magic of a barbershop where music and grooming intertwine.
